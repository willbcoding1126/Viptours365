Privacy

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how VIPTours365 collects, uses, shares, and retains customer information connected to presentation access and coordination services.

Information We Collect

Virtual Interactive Processing Services d/b/a VIPTours365 collects customer information necessary to process purchases, verify transactions, provide customer support, coordinate presentation access, and maintain compliance records.

Information collected may include customer name, phone number, email address, billing information, transaction information, signed agreements, verification records, support communications, and scheduling-related information.

How Information Is Used

Customer information may be used for payment authorization, customer verification, electronic agreement records, customer support, scheduling coordination, refund review, chargeback response, dispute response, compliance review, and legal recordkeeping.

Third-Party Sharing

VIPS may share necessary customer information with independent third-party providers for the purpose of scheduling or administering the required virtual presentation and related post-presentation processes.

VIPS does not sell customer personal information to unrelated third parties.

Record Retention

Customer records may be retained for customer service, billing, compliance, refund review, chargeback response, dispute response, and legal purposes.

Support

For privacy-related questions, contact VIPS support at info@viptours365.com or 833-383-5650.
