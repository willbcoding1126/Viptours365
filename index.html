Virtual Interactive Processing Services provides access to scheduled virtual experiences through a controlled digital service process. Customers purchase access to a live online presentation experience, supported by confirmation, coordination, and clear service disclosures before payment is processed.

The purchased service is access to the scheduled virtual presentation only. Any optional promotional opportunities introduced after completion are separate, independently managed, and fulfilled by third-party providers.

Service Summary

What is purchased: access to a scheduled virtual presentation experience.

How it is delivered: through a live online session conducted via Zoom or similar technology.

When service is complete: when the customer attends and completes the presentation.

  • No direct travel reservation is included in the original purchase.

Process

How It Works

A clear, controlled customer journey designed around disclosure, authorization, scheduling, and completion of the purchased virtual service.

01

Order & Disclosure

The customer reviews the service details and understands that the purchase is for access to a virtual presentation experience only.

02

Agreement & Authorization

The customer signs a digital agreement confirming product understanding, payment authorization, and required participation terms.

03

Scheduling & Confirmation

After payment is successful, the customer receives confirmation and instructions to schedule the virtual presentation.

04

Presentation Attendance

The customer attends the scheduled online presentation through Zoom or similar technology.

05

Service Completion

The purchased service is considered fully rendered once the customer completes the scheduled presentation.

06

Third-Party Opportunities

After completion, optional promotional opportunities may be introduced by independent third-party providers and are separate from the original purchase.

Important

Customer Disclosures

These disclosures are designed to remove confusion and clearly separate the purchased digital service from any optional third-party promotional opportunities.

Company Role

Virtual Interactive Processing Services is a digital service provider. VIPS does not provide travel services, accommodations, transportation, or guaranteed itineraries.

Third-Party Providers

After completion of the purchased service, customers may be introduced to optional promotional opportunities offered by independent third-party providers. Those services are separate from the original purchase.

Additional Costs

Any third-party promotional services may involve separate registration fees, taxes, resort fees, service fees, or provider-specific charges that are not included in the original purchase.

Refund Timing

Refund requests may be reviewed prior to completion of the scheduled presentation. Once the presentation is completed, the purchased service is considered fulfilled.

Customers purchase access to a scheduled virtual experience only. No direct travel, transportation, accommodations, or guaranteed reservations are included in the original transaction.
